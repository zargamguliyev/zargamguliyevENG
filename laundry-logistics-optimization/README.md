# Laundry Logistics Automation for Oasis Retreats Hotel Chain

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing and improving the logistics process between hotels and the centralized laundry facility within the **Oasis Retreats** hotel chain.

Due to rapid expansion, the hotel network opened **7 hotels in the region within two years**, which significantly increased the operational workload. The existing logistics process could no longer efficiently support the growing demand for linen transportation between hotels and the central laundry.

The goal of the project was to analyze the current logistics workflow and design a system that would automate delivery planning and improve transparency in linen movement.

---

# Business Problem

Interviews with employees and internal discussions revealed several operational issues:

- linen deliveries are planned **without considering hotel occupancy**
- hotels have **limited storage capacity**
- sometimes hotels experience **linen shortages**
- drivers follow **non-optimized routes**
- delivery arrival times are **unpredictable**
- discrepancies in linen quantities are detected **only during monthly inventory**
- lost items are difficult to trace

These issues lead to:

- inefficient logistics planning
- operational disruptions in hotels
- increased financial losses due to missing linen

---

# Solution

The proposed solution introduces a **logistics management system "L-Logistics"**.

Key elements of the solution:

• automated linen delivery planning  
• integration with the **hotel management system "O! Hotel"**  
• route planning for drivers  
• tracking linen movement between hotels and the laundry  
• improved transparency in inventory management

The system uses hotel occupancy data to forecast linen demand and improve delivery planning.
